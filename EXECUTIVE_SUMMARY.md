## Risk Assessment

### **High Risks & Mitigations**

#### **1. AI Model Performance**
- **Risk**: AI not accurate enough
- **Mitigation**: Use proven models (GPT-4), extensive testing, fallbacks

#### **2. User Adoption**
- **Risk**: Students/teachers don't adopt
- **Mitigation**: Excellent UX, teacher training, marketing

#### **3. Content Quality**
- **Risk**: Low-quality AI-generated content
- **Mitigation**: Expert review, quality metrics, teacher feedback

#### **4. Privacy/Security**
- **Risk**: Data breach or compliance failure
- **Mitigation**: Security-first design, audits, insurance

#### **5. Scalability**
- **Risk**: Platform can't handle growth
- **Mitigation**: Cloud-native, load testing, CDN

#### **6. Competition**
- **Risk**: Big players copy features
- **Mitigation**: Fast execution, strong brand, network effects
